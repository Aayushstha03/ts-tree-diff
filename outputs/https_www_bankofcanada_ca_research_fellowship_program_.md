Purpose
-------

The Fellowship Program is designed to encourage and foster excellence in research that relates to the Bank’s [core functions](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/core-functions/).

*   It promotes collaboration and partnerships between Bank researchers and leading academics at Canadian post-secondary institutions.
*   It also contributes to the education and development of Canada’s researchers in fields related to the Bank’s core functions.

See the Bank’s [current research priorities](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/research/).

[![Deputy Governor Rhys Mendes](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/RS1887_Rhys_Mendes_Fellowship-350x233.jpg "Deputy Governor Rhys Mendes")](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/RS1887_Rhys_Mendes_Fellowship.jpg "Deputy Governor Rhys Mendes")

> Collaboration between policymakers and academia is essential to progress in the public sphere—and from our perspective it fosters more innovative central banking. Creating those ties is the raison d’être for the Fellowship Program. It’s an ideal forum for building a shared experience that is enriching and valuable for all involved.”
> 
> [Rhys Mendes, Deputy Governor](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/profile/rhys-r-mendes/)
