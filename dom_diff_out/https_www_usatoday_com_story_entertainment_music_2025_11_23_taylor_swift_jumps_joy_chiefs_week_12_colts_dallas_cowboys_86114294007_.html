Taylor Swift jumps for joy as Travis Kelce and the Chiefs beat the Colts in Week 12

Bryan West
USA TODAY NETWORK

Taylor Swift is keeping her perfect attendance streak alive for every Kansas City Chiefs home game this season.

The pop superstar quietly made her way into the Arrowhead Stadium suite to support Travis Kelce as the Chiefs faced the Indianapolis Colts in a pivotal Week 12 matchup — just weeks before her new docuseries, "End of an Era," arrives Dec. 12 on Disney+.

According to fan page photos shared on social media from inside the stadium, Swift kept it casual, donning her hair in what looked to be two braids and an on-theme Kansas City Chiefs outfit featuring an off-white sweater with red accents.

Swift has adopted a notably lower profile, a shift from last season's viral tunnel moments and broadcast cutaways. At the last home game against the Commanders on Oct. 27, she was spotted in a red turtleneck and gold necklace, cheering Kelce's third-quarter touchdown beside Brittany Mahomes.

Announcers even nodded to her favorite number, joking, "We'll call this camera 13."

Another video shared by fans on X, formerly Twitter, showed Swift jumping up and down with excitement as Kelce and his team beat the Indianapolis Colts on Sunday.

"Taylor celebrating in the suite today!" the caption read. According to People magazine, the person she's seen shaking with joy during the football game appeared to be her father.

The Nov. 23 game kicked off under sunny skies with temperatures in the low 50s as Kansas City looked to revive its playoff hopes.

And that, they did, as the Kansas City Chiefs advanced their regular-season record to 6-5 with a thrilling overtime win against the Indianapolis Colts at Arrowhead Stadium in Week 12.

Despite a lackluster performance by star quarterback Patrick Mahomes — who completed 29 of his 46 passing attempts for 352 yards and threw one interception — Kansas City's offense managed to keep pace with Indianapolis thanks to veteran running back Kareem Hunt.

With its Week 12 victory over the Colts secured, Kansas City will look ahead to a matchup against the Dallas Cowboys on Thanksgiving Day.

Let's wait and see how Swift celebrates her lover then.
