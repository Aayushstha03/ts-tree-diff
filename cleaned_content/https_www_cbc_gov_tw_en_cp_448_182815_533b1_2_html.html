    PRESS RELEASE                                         Release Date: July 22, 2025

    Taipei Foreign Exchange Market June 2025

    The Taipei foreign exchange market covers all foreign exchange transactions made by authorized foreign exchange banks and offshore banking units. After eliminating double counting arising from interbank transactions, total net trading volume in the market amounted to US$963.7 billion in June 2025. The average daily turnover was US$45.9 billion.
