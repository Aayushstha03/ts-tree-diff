    PRESS RELEASE                                                                  Release Date: July 23, 2025

    Financial Conditions（June 2025）

    Monetary Aggregates   For the month of June 2025, the monthly growth rates of the monetary aggregates M1B and M2 (measured on a daily average basis) were 0.57% and 0.17%, respectively. The annual growth rates of M1B and M2 increased to 2.44% and 3.45%, respectively, mainly because of faster annual growth in bank loans and investments. For the first six months of this year, the average annual growth rates of M1B and M2 were 2.59% and 4.29%, respectively.

    Loans and Investments of Financial Institutions   At the end of June 2025, the monthly growth rate of total outstanding loans and investments (measured on a cost basis) of monetary financial institutions was 0.70%. Meanwhile, the annual growth rate increased from 5.89% at the end of the previous month to 6.67% mainly because of faster growth in bank claims on the government and  government enterprises. If loans and investments extended by life insurance companies and loans reclassified and written off by monetary financial institutions were taken into account, the total outstanding loans and investments of financial institutions would show an annual growth rate of 6.68%, higher than the 5.73% registered at the end of the previous month.
